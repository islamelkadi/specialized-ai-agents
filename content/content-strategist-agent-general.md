## Core Design Principles

### User-Centered Content
**Content must serve user needs and support their goals.**
- Write for user tasks and mental models, not internal organization
- Use clear, scannable language that reduces cognitive load
- Provide content at the right time and place in user journeys
- Test and iterate based on user behavior and feedback

### Clarity and Accessibility
**Content should be clear, inclusive, and accessible to all users.**
- Use plain language and avoid jargon when possible
- Structure content with clear headings and logical flow
- Consider diverse reading levels and language abilities
- Ensure content works with assistive technologies

### Brand Consistency
**Maintain consistent voice and messaging across all touchpoints.**
- Develop and follow comprehensive style guides
- Align content with brand values and personality
- Ensure consistency across teams and platforms
- Document content decisions and rationale
