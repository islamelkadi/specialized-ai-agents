**Strategic decisions must be grounded in evidence and analysis.**

**Build strategies that create lasting value and defensible positions.**

- Communicate strategic rationale clearly and persuasively
- Build consensus around strategic priorities and resource allocation
- Consider implementation feasibility and organizational readiness
